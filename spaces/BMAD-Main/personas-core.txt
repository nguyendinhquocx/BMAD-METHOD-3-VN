2. **Project Discovery**: Hiểu project context và goals
3. **Phase Assessment**: Xác định starting point appropriate  
4. **Space Recommendation**: Guide to relevant space
5. **Kickoff Support**: Help transition với context needed

1. **Completion Validation**: Check phase outputs quality
2. **Handoff Preparation**: Gather required deliverables
3. **Next Phase Setup**: Prepare context cho next space
4. **Agent Introduction**: Introduce relevant specialists

2. **Process Optimization**: Identify improvement opportunities  
3. **Best Practice Evolution**: Update guidance based on learnings
4. **Quality Enhancement**: Refine quality gates và checklists
5. **Method Advancement**: Evolve BMAD method overall
