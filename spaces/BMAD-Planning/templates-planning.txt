## üìù Meeting & Session Templates

### Brainstorming Session Template

```markdown
# Brainstorming Session: {Topic}

## Session Information
- **Date:** {Date}
- **Facilitator:** Mary (Analyst)
- **Participants:** {Participant list}
- **Duration:** {Estimated time}
- **Session Goal:** {What we want to achieve}

## Pre-Session Setup
### Context Setting
- **Current Situation:** {Brief description}
- **Challenge/Opportunity:** {What we're exploring}
- **Constraints:** {Any known limitations}

### Ground Rules
- No judgment during idea generation
- Build on others' ideas ("Yes, and...")
- Quantity over quality initially
- Stay focused on the topic
- All ideas get captured

## Session Agenda
### 1. Problem Framing (10 minutes)
- Restate the challenge clearly
- Ensure shared understanding

### 2. Divergent Thinking (30 minutes)
- Individual idea generation (5 minutes)
- Round-robin sharing (15 minutes)
- Building on ideas (10 minutes)

### 3. Idea Expansion (20 minutes)
- "What if..." scenarios
- Analogical thinking exercises
- Assumption challenging

### 4. Initial Convergence (15 minutes)
- Group similar ideas
- Identify most promising concepts
- Note questions for further exploration

### 5. Next Steps (5 minutes)
- Summarize key insights
- Identify research needs
- Plan follow-up actions

## Idea Capture Template
### Idea: {Idea Name}
- **Description:** {Brief explanation}
- **Potential Value:** {Why this could be valuable}
- **Challenges:** {Potential obstacles}
- **Next Steps:** {What to explore further}

## Post-Session Deliverables
- Consolidated idea list
- Prioritized concept areas
- Research questions identified
- Recommended next steps
```
