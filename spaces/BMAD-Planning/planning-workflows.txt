# BMAD Planning Workflows - Quy Trình Làm Việc Chi Tiết Giai Đoạn Lập Kế Hoạch

---

## 🎯 Overview - Tổng Quan Quy Trình

### Planning Phase Mission
**Transform ý tưởng ban đầu thành comprehensive, validated product requirements sẵn sàng cho architecture design.**

### Key Success Outcomes
- ✅ **Clear Problem Definition**: Well-articulated problem statement với validated user need
- ✅ **Comprehensive PRD**: Complete Product Requirements Document với all necessary detail
- ✅ **Validated MVP Scope**: Clear boundaries của what's included/excluded from MVP
- ✅ **User Understanding**: Deep insights about target users và their needs
- ✅ **Market Intelligence**: Understanding của competitive landscape và opportunity
- ✅ **Epic Structure**: Logical breakdown của development work
- ✅ **Architecture Foundation**: Technical constraints và preferences identified

---

## 🚀 Workflow 1: New Project Planning (Greenfield)

### Dành cho: Dự án hoàn toàn mới từ ý tưởng ban đầu

### Phase 1: Discovery & Brainstorming (Tuần 1)

#### Step 1.1: Initial Context Gathering (Day 1)
**Agent**: Analyst (Mary)  
**Duration**: 1-2 hours

**Activities**:
- Initial user interview về project vision
- Capture initial requirements, constraints, assumptions
- Identify key stakeholders và decision makers
- Set expectations về planning process

**Deliverables**:
- Initial context document
- Stakeholder map
- Planning timeline

#### Step 1.2: Brainstorming Session (Day 1-2)
**Agent**: Analyst (Mary)  
**Duration**: 2-3 hours (có thể split thành multiple sessions)

**Pre-Session Prep**:
- Review initial context
- Prepare brainstorming environment
- Identify facilitation techniques to use

**Session Structure**:
```markdown
**Brainstorming Agenda** (90-120 minutes):
├── Problem Framing (15 min)
├── Divergent Ideation (45 min)
├── Idea Building (30 min)  
├── Initial Convergence (20 min)
└── Next Steps Planning (10 min)
```

**Facilitation Techniques**:
- "What if..." scenarios for expansion
- Analogical thinking ("Like X but for Y")
- First principles breakdown
- SCAMPER method application
- "Yes, and..." building exercises

**Deliverables**:
- Brainstorming session summary
- Top 5-7 concept areas identified
- Key insights about user needs
- Research questions for further investigation

#### Step 1.3: Concept Evaluation (Day 2-3)
**Agent**: Analyst (Mary)  
**Duration**: 4-6 hours

**Activities**:
- Evaluate concepts against feasibility, value, alignment
- Prioritize concepts based on evaluation criteria
- Identify top 2-3 concepts for deeper exploration
- Plan research needs for concept validation

**Evaluation Framework**:
```markdown
**Concept Evaluation Matrix**:
| Concept | User Value | Feasibility | Market Size | Alignment | Total Score |
|---------|------------|-------------|-------------|-----------|-------------|
| Concept 1 | 8/10 | 7/10 | 9/10 | 8/10 | 32/40 |
| Concept 2 | 9/10 | 6/10 | 7/10 | 9/10 | 31/40 |
```

**Deliverables**:
- Concept evaluation report
- Prioritized concept list
- Research requirements for top concepts

### Phase 2: Research & Validation (Tuần 2)

#### Step 2.1: Research Planning (Day 4)
**Agent**: Analyst (Mary)  
**Duration**: 2-3 hours

**Activities**:
- Create detailed research prompt cho top concepts
- Define research questions, methodology, sources
- Set success criteria cho research phase
- Plan research timeline và resource allocation

**Research Planning Template**:
```markdown
**Research Plan**:
├── **Primary Questions**: What do we need to validate?
├── **Research Areas**: Market, Competition, Technology, Users
├── **Methodology**: How will we gather information?
├── **Sources**: Where will we look for answers?
├── **Timeline**: When will research be completed?
└── **Success Criteria**: How do we know when we have enough?
```

**Deliverables**:
- Comprehensive research prompt
- Research methodology document
- Resource requirements

#### Step 2.2: Research Execution (Day 4-8)
**Agent**: Analyst (Mary) hoặc Specialized Research Agent  
**Duration**: 1-2 weeks

**Research Focus Areas**:

##### Market Research
- Market size và growth potential
- Target user segments và needs
- Current solutions và their limitations
- Pricing models và revenue opportunities

##### Competitive Analysis
- Direct và indirect competitors
- Competitive strengths và weaknesses
- Market positioning opportunities
- Differentiation strategies

##### Technical Feasibility
- Technology options và constraints
- Integration requirements
- Development complexity assessment
- Resource requirements estimation

##### User Validation
- User interview planning và execution
- Survey design và distribution
- User journey mapping
- Pain point validation

**Deliverables**:
- Market research report
- Competitive analysis summary
- Technical feasibility assessment
- User validation findings

#### Step 2.3: Research Synthesis (Day 9)
**Agent**: Analyst (Mary)  
**Duration**: 4-6 hours

**Activities**:
- Synthesize all research findings
- Validate hoặc invalidate initial concepts
- Identify refined concept direction
- Prepare insights cho PRD development

**Synthesis Framework**:
```markdown
**Research Synthesis Structure**:
├── **Key Findings**: What did we learn?
├── **Validated Assumptions**: What was confirmed?
├── **Invalidated Assumptions**: What was disproven?
├── **New Insights**: What surprised us?
├── **Recommended Direction**: What should we build?
└── **Outstanding Questions**: What still needs answers?
```

**Deliverables**:
- Research synthesis report
- Refined concept definition
- User persona drafts
- Market opportunity assessment

### Phase 3: PRD Development (Tuần 3)

#### Step 3.1: PRD Planning (Day 10)
**Agent**: PM (John)  
**Duration**: 2-3 hours

**Handoff Activities**:
- Review all research findings với Analyst
- Understand validated concept direction
- Clarify success metrics và business goals
- Plan PRD development approach

**PRD Planning Checklist**:
- [ ] Research findings reviewed và understood
- [ ] Business goals clarity confirmed
- [ ] Success metrics defined
- [ ] User personas validated
- [ ] Technical constraints identified
- [ ] Stakeholder input requirements planned

**Deliverables**:
- PRD development plan
- Success metrics definition
- Stakeholder engagement strategy

#### Step 3.2: PRD Drafting (Day 10-12)
**Agent**: PM (John)  
**Duration**: 2-3 days

**PRD Development Sequence**:
```markdown
**Day 10**: Foundation
├── Executive Summary
├── Problem Statement
├── Business Goals & Success Metrics
└── Market Context

**Day 11**: Requirements
├── User Analysis (Personas & Journeys)
├── Functional Requirements
├── Non-Functional Requirements
└── UI/UX Requirements

**Day 12**: Planning
├── MVP Definition & Scope
├── Epic Breakdown
├── Technical Constraints
└── Implementation Guidance
```

**Quality Standards**:
- All requirements testable và verifiable
- User stories follow consistent format
- Acceptance criteria clearly defined
- Dependencies identified và documented
- Technical constraints realistic và specific

**Deliverables**:
- Complete PRD draft
- Epic breakdown với priorities
- MVP scope definition
- Technical constraints document

#### Step 3.3: PRD Validation (Day 13-14)
**Agent**: PM (John)  
**Duration**: 1-2 days

**Validation Process**:
```markdown
**PRD Validation Steps**:
├── **Self-Review**: PM checklist validation
├── **Analyst Review**: Alignment với research findings
├── **Technical Review**: Feasibility assessment
├── **Stakeholder Review**: Business alignment
└── **Final Integration**: Incorporate all feedback
```

**PM Checklist Review Categories**:
- Problem Definition & Context
- MVP Scope Definition  
- User Experience Requirements
- Functional Requirements
- Non-Functional Requirements
- Epic & Story Structure
- Technical Guidance
- Cross-Functional Requirements
- Clarity & Communication

**Deliverables**:
- Validated PRD với checklist sign-off
- Stakeholder approval documentation
- Outstanding issues list với resolution plan

### Phase 4: Planning Completion (Day 15)

#### Step 4.1: Final Quality Review
**Agents**: Both Analyst (Mary) và PM (John)  
**Duration**: 2-3 hours

**Joint Review Activities**:
- Final consistency check across all documents
- Ensure research insights properly reflected in PRD
- Validate epic structure supports MVP goals
- Confirm technical constraints realistic
- Verify stakeholder alignment maintained

#### Step 4.2: Architecture Handoff Preparation  
**Agent**: PM (John)  
**Duration**: 1-2 hours

**Handoff Package Preparation**:
```markdown
**Architecture Handoff Package**:
├── **PRD.md** (validated)
├── **Research-Summary.md** (key insights)
├── **User-Personas.md** (detailed personas)
├── **Epic-Breakdown.md** (development structure)
├── **Technical-Constraints.md** (architecture inputs)
├── **Market-Context.md** (competitive landscape)
└── **Handoff-Notes.md** (context for architecture team)
```

**Deliverables**:
- Complete handoff package
- Architecture phase success criteria
- Communication plan với architecture team

## 🔄 Workflow 2: Existing Product Enhancement

### Dành cho: Enhancement của existing product với new features

### Modified Planning Approach

#### Phase 1: Current State Analysis (3-4 days)
- **Existing Product Audit**: Current features, user feedback, performance metrics
- **Gap Analysis**: What's missing vs user needs
- **Technical Debt Assessment**: Current architecture limitations
- **User Research**: Additional needs và pain points

#### Phase 2: Enhancement Strategy (3-4 days)  
- **Opportunity Prioritization**: Which enhancements provide most value
- **Integration Planning**: How new features fit với existing product
- **Migration Strategy**: How to transition existing users
- **Resource Assessment**: Development effort vs current team capacity

#### Phase 3: Enhanced PRD Development (5-7 days)
- **Existing System Integration**: How new features interact với current system
- **Backward Compatibility**: Ensuring existing users aren't disrupted
- **Phased Rollout Plan**: Strategy for gradual feature introduction
- **Success Metrics**: How to measure enhancement success

## 🚨 Workflow 3: Pivot/Major Change Management

### Dành cho: Major changes discovered during planning process

### Change Management Process

#### Phase 1: Change Impact Assessment (1-2 days)
**Activities**:
- Identify root cause của change requirement
- Assess impact on completed work
- Evaluate alternative approaches
- Stakeholder communication và alignment

#### Phase 2: Path Forward Evaluation (1-2 days)
**Options Analysis**:
- **Option A**: Minor Adjustment - Limited changes to current approach
- **Option B**: Major Pivot - Significant direction change  
- **Option C**: Scope Reduction - Remove features to stay on track
- **Option D**: Resource Augmentation - Add resources to handle complexity

#### Phase 3: Change Implementation (Variable)
**Activities**:
- Update all affected documents
- Recommunicate với stakeholders
- Adjust timeline và resource plans
- Validate new direction với research

#### Phase 4: Change Validation (1 day)
**Activities**:
- Ensure all documents consistent
- Stakeholder alignment confirmation
- Risk assessment of new direction
- Success metrics adjustment

- **Timeline Adherence**: 95% of milestones met on time
- **Stakeholder Satisfaction**: 4.5/5 rating on planning process
- **Document Quality**: All checklists pass với >90% score
- **Research Coverage**: All key questions answered

#### Outcome Metrics  
- **Architecture Phase Success**: Architecture documents completed on time
- **Requirement Stability**: <10% requirement changes during architecture

#### Efficiency Metrics
- **Planning Velocity**: Time from start to validated PRD
- **Rework Rate**: Percentage của planning work that needed revision
- **Stakeholder Cycles**: Number of review cycles needed for approval
- **Knowledge Transfer**: Architecture team confidence level với handoff

### Continuous Improvement Framework

#### Weekly Retrospectives
- What worked well trong planning process?
- What could be improved?
- What should we start/stop/continue doing?
- How can we better prepare for architecture phase?

#### Monthly Process Review
- Review planning success metrics
- Identify process improvement opportunities  
- Update templates và workflows based on learnings
- Share best practices across teams

## 🎯 Planning Workflow Decision Tree

```
❓ What type of planning project?
├── New Product (Greenfield)
│   ├── Complex/Uncertain → Full 3-week planning process
│   └── Simple/Clear → Shortened 2-week planning process
├── Product Enhancement
│   ├── Major Feature Addition → Enhanced planning process
│   └── Minor Feature Addition → Streamlined planning process  
├── Product Pivot
│   ├── Technology Change → Focus on technical research
│   └── Market Change → Focus on market research
└── Emergency/Urgent
    ├── Fast-track planning process (1 week)
    └── Minimum viable planning với ongoing refinement
```
